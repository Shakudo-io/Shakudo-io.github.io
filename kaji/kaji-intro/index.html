What is Kaji

Kaji (鍛冶) is Shakudo's AI-powered agent platform. It lets your team build, automate, and run complex tasks using plain language — no code required.

With Kaji, you describe what you want done and the AI agent takes care of the rest: writing code, querying databases, managing tasks, generating content, and more.

What makes Kaji different?​

Kaji isn't just a chatbot. It's a full agent platform that can:

  • Take action — Kaji doesn't just suggest; it executes. It runs code, edits files, searches databases, and interacts with your tools directly.
  • Remember — Kaji retains knowledge across conversations, so you don't have to repeat yourself.
  • Automate — Schedule recurring tasks (like daily summaries or weekly reports) that run on their own.
  • Delegate — Kaji can split complex work into parallel subtasks and coordinate the results.
  • Browse — Kaji can open a real browser, navigate websites, fill forms, and capture screenshots for you.

How do you use Kaji?​

Kaji is primarily accessed through KajiChat, a modern web-based chat interface. Open KajiChat from your Shakudo dashboard, start a conversation, and describe what you need.

Pull our Q1 sales data from Dremio and summarize it in a table
Create a ClickUp task for each bug in this list and assign them to the engineering team
Draft a weekly status update based on the tasks completed this week
